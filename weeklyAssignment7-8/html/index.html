Who we are

The N-Frnds unique “Last Mile” hybrid, multi-product distribution network for emerging markets, connects consumer goods brands, insurers and financial institutions with small retailers and consumers unlocking growth.

Our Hybrid platform combines technology with our network of sales agents who “plug-in” to the current traditional distribution network. We have achieved global scale with over 20M users on the platform (mobile, web, USSD), a proven business model, 21 patents, successful live networks in South East Asia and Africa.

Customer stories

Digitized Agriculture

N-Frnds has partnered with the Rwandan Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Resources to digitize services and processes, making the opportunities of SaaS accessible to more than 80% of the country’s population.

Branchless Banking

N-Frnds has partnered with Indonesian Bank, BTPN Wow, to provide one of the world’s largest branchless banking solutions in the world, bringing over 3.5 million mass market users into the financial system.

Accessible Government

N-Frnds has partnered with RwandaOnline to make over 100 government services accessible to Rwanda’s 12 million citizens and small businesses.
